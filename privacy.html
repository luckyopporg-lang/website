Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Lucky Opportunity Organization (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

Information we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us — such as your name, email address, phone number, and any message you send through our contact or volunteer sign-up forms. We do not collect payment information through this site.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiries, coordinate volunteer and donation opportunities, and keep you informed about our events and programs. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form submissions

Forms on this site are processed by Netlify, our hosting provider, which stores submissions securely so we can read and respond to them. Please review Netlify’s privacy practices for details on their handling of data.

Cookies & analytics

This site does not use advertising cookies. If we add basic analytics in the future to understand site traffic, we will update this policy accordingly.

Your choices

You may request that we update or delete the personal information you have shared with us at any time by contacting us using the details below.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email luckyopporg@gmail.com or call (510) 566-1191.
