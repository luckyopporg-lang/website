Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2026

Lucky Opportunity Organization is committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level.

What we do

  • Use semantic HTML and clear heading structure for screen readers.
  • Provide keyboard navigation and visible focus indicators.
  • Maintain strong color contrast for readable text.
  • Include descriptive labels for forms and meaningful alternative text for images.
  • Respect reduced-motion preferences.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We continue to review and improve the site, and we welcome feedback that helps us do better.

Need help or found a barrier?

If you experience any difficulty accessing part of this site, please let us know and we will do our best to assist and to fix the issue. Email luckyopporg@gmail.com or call (510) 566-1191.
