Smash Baseball team photo
📅 March 7, 2026

Smash Baseball Fundraiser

We came together to support our local Smash 12U youth baseball team as they raised funds to make the trip to Cooperstown for their tournament. From the kids rallying the community with handmade signs to a packed fundraiser night, it was all about helping these young athletes chase their dream on the road.

Halloween Trunk-or-Treat & Car Show

Our biggest Halloween night yet — glowing inflatables, a classic car & lowrider show, food and vendor booths, and trick-or-treating for families across the community. Costumes, candy, and lots of smiles all around.

📅 April 4, 2026

Easter Eggstravaganza & Show & Shine

A packed spring day at 6151 H Street in Sacramento — a huge community Easter egg hunt for the kids paired with a Show & Shine car show, plus a raffle, balloon making, food, and vendors. Hundreds of families came out for the fun.
